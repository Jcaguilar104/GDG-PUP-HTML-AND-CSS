My Hobby: Reading

Top 3 Things I Need:

  • Book or Reading Devices
  • Comfortable Bed or Chair
  • Coffee

Steps to Get Started:

  1. I always begin by selecting the book or story I want to read.
  2. Once I've made my selection, I prepare my chair or bed for a comfortable reading experience.
  3. While preparing my reading space, I also brew a cup of coffee to enjoy while I read.
  4. Finally, I settle comfortably into my bed and begin reading.
